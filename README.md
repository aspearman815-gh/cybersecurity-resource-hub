# 🔐 Cybersecurity Resource Hub

Welcome to the **Cybersecurity Resource Hub** — a curated collection of events, career opportunities, certifications, and professional development resources for anyone interested in building or advancing a career in cybersecurity.

This repository organizes high-quality cybersecurity information in one place to make it easier to learn, grow, and stay informed.

---

## 📅 Cybersecurity Events
Stay updated with upcoming conferences, webinars, workshops, and trainings.

Types of events included:
- Cybersecurity conferences
- Virtual and in-person workshops
- Webinars and training sessions
- Capture the Flag (CTF) competitions
- Community meetups and student-focused events

🔗 **Events Directory:** `events/`

---

## 🎓 Certifications & Learning Paths
Explore essential cybersecurity certifications and learning pathways for beginners and professionals.

Included:
- Entry-level certifications (Security+, CC, Google Cybersecurity)
- Intermediate certifications (CySA+, SSCP, CEH)
- Advanced certifications (CISSP, CISM, CASP+)
- Free training resources
- Study guides & recommended learning tracks

🔗 **Certifications Directory:** `certifications/`
